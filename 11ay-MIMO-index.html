IEEE 802.11ay MIMO Protocols

BTI A-BFT seq is an introduction to 11ay initial access.

SU-MIMO-beamforming-training introduces how an STA has further sector level beam search and refines the beams.

MU-MIMO-beamforming-training introduces how a group of STAs have further sector level beam search and refines their beams to form MU-MIMO group with several MIMO configurations.

Hybrid-mimo-beamforming is an introduction to 11ay hybrid beamforming protocol/MIMO Sounding.

MIMO-Channel-Access is an introduction to 11ay MIMO Channel Access.
